Mechanical Engineering

Our mechanical engineering efforts focus on creating a durable, accessible, and modular structure:

  • Frame & Structure: Polycarbonate panels reinforced with metal brackets. Dimensions: 20" x 20" x 25".
  • Dispensing Mechanism: Four spiral springs driven by servo motors, allowing bi-directional rotation for dispensing and restocking.
  • Storage Compartments: Four snack sections plus a hidden circuitry compartment.
  • Retrieval Door: Hinged flap to allow access while preventing theft.
  • Accessibility: Lockable front and back access panels for reloading and repairs.
