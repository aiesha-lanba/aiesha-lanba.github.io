Electrical Engineering

The electrical system is designed for simplicity and reliability, powered by an Arduino Mega 2560 Rev3:

  • Servo Motors: Four 5V high-torque servos controlled by the Arduino.
  • IR Sensor: Detects successful dispensing and enables retry logic.
  • Keypad: 1x4 membrane keypad for item selection.
  • LED Strips: Improve visibility and indicate stock status.
  • Power Supply: 9V AC-to-DC adapter with onboard buck converter for 5V regulation.
  • Reload Switch: Toggles motor direction for restocking items.
